Program Overview

Brigades build participatory power in their cities by holding regular civic hack nights and events, advocating for open data, and deploying apps. Brigades celebrate and share their successes with the Brigade network to further the global civic innovation movement.

Who We Are

Brigade Network Members. Every Brigade needs leadership, core team members, regular hack nights, and online chanelles where citizens can engage. Brigade Official Chapters. As Brigades build, they set startegic plans organized around the Code for America Principles of Digital Government. Brigade Institutions. One key way Brigades accomplish their goals is by partnering with local government officials and other community organizations in their cities.
