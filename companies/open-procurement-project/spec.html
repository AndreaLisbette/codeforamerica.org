THE PROJECT Summary of Terms

This cover page is a simplified summary of all the major terms of the RFP. Sections describe the project, requirements, and application process in a single page.

This section improves typical practice in RFPs by centralizing and condensing all the terms into a concise summary. The intention is to allow potential responders to quickly determine high level requirements and determine whether they should participate in the RFP process. It also significantly streamlines customization - cities simply change the details in this section, rather than having to edit a large number of terms throughout the entire document.
Requesting City: [City Name] (the “City”)
Project Summary: This is a Request for Proposal (“RFP”) for a contractor (the “Contractor”) to provide creative services in developing the visual identity of the redesigned website, as a first step to building a collected set of standardized website design elements - a “pattern library”.
Project Stages: Work will proceed in three stages:
  • Discovery - Contractor reviews materials, conducts workshops and proposes design approaches via mood boards or other methods (approx. 2-4 weeks).
  • Design - Contractor does 3 sequential iterations of major design elements including logos, colors, typography, recommended photograph styles, sample icons [etc]. (approx. 3-6 weeks).
  • Delivery - Contractor finalizes designs and delivers a complete visual identity guide as a PDF and source files. (approx. 1-2 weeks).
Project Deadline: All work related to the project must be completed by [time and date], unless otherwise agreed by City.
APPLICATIONS:
Due Date: On or before [time and date] (the “Due Date”).
Delivery Method: Deliver [__] printed copies of your response to this RFP to the Delivery Address below. [Please also deliver an electronic copy to [email address]]. All responses must be received on or before the Due Date in order to be considered.
